About RCME

A focused institution for management and entrepreneurial learning.

RCME supports students who want a practical, career-aware, and disciplined PGDM learning environment in Bangalore.

Who we are

Regional College of Management & Entrepreneurship is positioned around management education with attention to industry awareness, communication, decision-making, and student development.

The campus context at Mudugurki, Devanahalli, Bangalore gives students access to a focused academic environment while staying connected to one of India's active business cities.

Focus areas

  • Management foundation
  • Entrepreneurial mindset
  • Professional communication
  • Student mentoring
  • Campus-based learning support
